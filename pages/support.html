Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions and issues

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.

If you can't access your purchased games, first ensure you're logged in with the correct account. Check your download history in your profile, and verify that your payment was processed successfully. If the issue persists, contact our billing support team.

To report a bug, go to our bug report form in the support section. Include detailed information about the issue, your system specifications, and steps to reproduce the problem. Our technical team will investigate and provide updates on the status.

GameHub requires Windows 10 or later, at least 8GB RAM, and a DirectX 11 compatible graphics card. For the best experience, we recommend 16GB RAM and a dedicated graphics card. Check individual game pages for specific requirements.

To cancel your subscription, go to your account settings and navigate to the "Billing & Subscriptions" section. Click on "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Game licenses are tied to the purchasing account and cannot be transferred to another account for security and anti-fraud reasons. However, you can share your account with family members through our Family Sharing feature.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the reset link
  4. Create a new strong password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How can I enable two-factor authentication?

To enable 2FA for better security:

  • Go to Account Settings â†’ Security
  • Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  • Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
  • Enter the verification code

We recommend using Google Authenticator or Authy.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days:

  • Navigate to Profile Settings
  • Click "Edit Profile"
  • Enter your new desired username
  • Save changes (username must be unique)
