How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the reset link
  4. Create a new strong password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How can I enable two-factor authentication?

To enable 2FA for better security:

  • Go to Account Settings → Security
  • Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  • Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
  • Enter the verification code

We recommend using Google Authenticator or Authy.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days:

  • Navigate to Profile Settings
  • Click "Edit Profile"
  • Enter your new desired username
  • Save changes (username must be unique)
